Privacy

Effective 21 July 2026

What is processed

For a market lookup or bounds preview, Conviction processes the market reference, selected outcome, total risk budget, and maximum price. A final position card also processes the public Polygon deposit-wallet address and an optional note. Receipt verification processes the submitted transaction hash, order ID, intent, and intent hash.

What the application does not request

Never submit a seed phrase, private key, bearer token, CLOB credential, reusable signature, raw authorization, or live-trading confirmation. The browser does not connect to a wallet, sign, approve, or broadcast.

Storage and infrastructure

Conviction has no application database, user accounts, analytics SDK, advertising tracker, or cookies. The public preview and verifier temporarily retain an IP-shaped client identifier and request count in a warm server instance to limit abuse; those buckets are bounded and are not written to an application database. Hosting, x402 facilitation, Polymarket APIs, and Polygon RPC infrastructure may retain ordinary request, operational, or payment logs under their own policies. A successful paid service notification contains only fixed settlement metadata—service name, amount, network, transaction hash, and time—not the wallet, note, or request body.
