Terms

Effective 21 July 2026

Deliverable

The free web flow previews objective OPEN market and exposure data. The paid POST /api/service route costs 0.05 USD₮0 and delivers one ready-to-sign bounded OPEN card. The free POST /api/manage-preview route previews one source-bound manager action. The paid POST /api/manage route costs 0.10 USD₮0 and delivers either an exact-share CLOSE card or a post-only GTD TAKE_PROFIT card, selected explicitly by action. Neither response alone is a fill, wallet signature, broadcast, financial recommendation, or promise of execution.

User decision and eligibility

For OPEN, you choose the market, YES or NO outcome, total budget, maximum price, wallet, and whether to proceed. For CLOSE, you choose exact whole shares and a minimum price. For TAKE_PROFIT, you choose exact whole shares, a target price, and a venue expiry. Third-party venue terms and regional eligibility apply. Do not use Conviction where prediction-market activity is unavailable or prohibited, and do not bypass an employer or organization’s security policy.

Execution and approvals

The website remains an OPEN preview and verification surface. The public buyer agent/CLI can execute an exact OPEN, CLOSE, or TAKE_PROFIT card through the official Polymarket plugin and your Agentic Wallet in the same session, without asking you to type plugin commands. Service-payment consent and trade consent are separate; paying 0.05 or 0.10 USD₮0 never authorizes a trade. Canceling a TAKE_PROFIT requires its own exact-order confirmation. A new Polymarket deposit wallet may require five broad token approvals. Review them on a dedicated low-balance wallet. Polygon gas, order principal, and venue fees are separate from the Conviction service fee.

Position Manager source boundary

CLOSE and TAKE_PROFIT require a prior OPEN settlement that Conviction independently reverifies, plus a fresh seller balance and standard V2 approval. A legacy v2/v3 OPEN is retrospective provenance, not a consumable lot or one-time authorization. Seller-owned balance is the authority to sell. TAKE_PROFIT is one bounded post-only GTD order, not a recurring strategy, stop loss, autonomous re-entry, or portfolio-management grant.

No performance guarantee

Market data, liquidity, venue availability, fills, resolution, and profit are not guaranteed. An OPEN FAK order may fill partially or not at all. An exact-share CLOSE FOK must fill the requested shares at or above the minimum price or produce no fill. A TAKE_PROFIT can remain open, fill partially across transactions, expire, or race with an exact-order cancellation; authenticated status is not mislabeled as an on-chain fill. Conviction fails closed when its supported market, quote, source, balance, approval, token, budget, price, proceeds, wallet, order, trade, or receipt invariants do not hold.
