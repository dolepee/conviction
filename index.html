Hard boundary

Conviction executes intent. It does not manufacture it.

No signals, no hidden sizing, no custody, no leverage. You choose the market, side, and economic bound; the official Polymarket plugin and your Agentic Wallet handle signing.

New Polymarket deposit wallets use max token allowances and blanket outcome-token approvals to official exchange contracts. Conviction discloses that setup and recommends a dedicated low-balance wallet.

Polymarket V2 applies venue fees at match time rather than inside the signed order. Conviction therefore derives a conservative fee reserve, requires the dedicated wallet balance to cap exposure, and verifies principal, fee, and total debit independently after settlement.
